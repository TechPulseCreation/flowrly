Kids Play Area

A safe, fun space for your little ones while you enjoy your dining experience.

Safe & Supervised Fun

  • Supervised play area with certified childcare staff
  • Open daily from 5PM to 9PM
  • For children ages 3-10 years old
  • Educational toys, games, and activities
  • Special kids menu available upon request

Safety First

Our play area features soft flooring, rounded edges, and regular sanitation. All staff are background-checked and CPR-certified. Parents must remain on premises while children are in the play area.
